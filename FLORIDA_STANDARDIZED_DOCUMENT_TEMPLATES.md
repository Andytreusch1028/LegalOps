# Florida Standardized Document Templates

## 🏛️ **YES - Florida Has Pre-Approved, Standardized Document Templates**

Florida offers a comprehensive system of pre-approved, standardized real estate and legal document templates that are developed and maintained by **Florida Realtors®** in collaboration with **The Florida Bar** to ensure compliance with state laws and regulations.

---

## 📋 **FLORIDA REALTORS® / FLORIDA BAR STANDARDIZED FORMS**

### **🏠 Residential Real Estate Forms**

#### **1. Residential Contract for Sale and Purchase (FloridaRealtors/FloridaBar-7)**
- **Purpose**: Standard residential property purchase agreements
- **Features**: 
  - Purchase price and financing terms
  - Closing details and contingencies
  - Inspection and appraisal provisions
  - Default and remedy clauses
- **Compliance**: Approved by Florida Bar and Florida Realtors®
- **Usage**: Most common form for residential transactions

#### **2. "AS IS" Residential Contract for Sale and Purchase (FloridaRealtors/FloridaBar-ASIS-7)**
- **Purpose**: Residential sales where seller makes no repairs
- **Features**:
  - Property sold in current condition
  - Buyer right to cancel if unsatisfied
  - No seller repair obligations
  - Inspection rights maintained
- **Compliance**: Approved by Florida Bar and Florida Realtors®
- **Usage**: Distressed properties, investment properties

#### **3. Comprehensive Rider to Contract for Sale and Purchase (CR-7)**
- **Purpose**: Additional clauses for standard contracts
- **Features**:
  - Specific contingencies
  - Special conditions
  - Custom terms and provisions
  - Additional disclosures
- **Compliance**: Approved by Florida Bar and Florida Realtors®
- **Usage**: Complex transactions requiring special terms

#### **4. Vacant Land Contract (VAC-14xxxx)**
- **Purpose**: Undeveloped land transactions
- **Features**:
  - Land-specific terms
  - Zoning and development provisions
  - Environmental considerations
  - Utility access provisions
- **Compliance**: Approved by Florida Bar and Florida Realtors®
- **Usage**: Raw land, development properties

## ⚖️ **UPL COMPLIANCE FOR FLORIDA FORMS**

### **✅ UPL-Compliant Usage**

#### **1. Template-Based Document Creation**
- **Standardized Forms**: Use only approved Florida Realtors®/Florida Bar forms
- **Information Collection**: Collect factual information from clients
- **Document Assembly**: Assemble documents based on client information
- **No Legal Review**: Don't review documents for legal accuracy
- **Client Responsibility**: Clients responsible for legal review

#### **2. Clear Disclaimers Required**
```
"LEGAL DISCLAIMER: This document is provided as a template only. We are not attorneys and do not provide legal advice. This document should be reviewed by a qualified attorney before use. We recommend consulting with an attorney for any legal questions or concerns."
```

#### **3. Attorney Referral Network**
- **Complex Legal Issues**: Refer to qualified attorneys
- **Custom Document Needs**: Refer to qualified attorneys
- **Legal Disputes**: Refer to qualified attorneys
- **Regulatory Issues**: Refer to qualified attorneys
