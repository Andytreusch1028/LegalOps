### 🎯 **Core Business Startup Needs Analysis**

Based on research and industry best practices, here are the essential services every business needs at startup:

## 📋 **Essential Startup Services**

### **1. Federal Requirements**
- **EIN (Employer Identification Number)** - IRS requirement for tax filing, banking, hiring
- **Federal Licenses** - Industry-specific (alcohol, agriculture, firearms, etc.)
- **Beneficial Ownership Information (BOI)** - FinCEN reporting (2024 requirement)

### **2. State Requirements**
- **Business Entity Formation** - LLC, Corporation, Partnership registration
- **State Tax Registration** - Sales tax, employer taxes, business taxes
- **Professional Licenses** - Industry-specific state licenses
- **State Business License** - General business operation license

### **3. Local Requirements**
- **City Business License** - Local operation permit
- **County Permits** - Zoning, health, safety permits
- **Home Occupation Permit** - For home-based businesses
- **Sign Permits** - Business signage approval

### **4. Industry-Specific Requirements**
- **Health Permits** - Restaurants, healthcare, beauty services
- **Building Permits** - Construction, renovations
- **Environmental Permits** - Manufacturing, waste disposal
- **Professional Licenses** - Medical, legal, accounting, real estate
