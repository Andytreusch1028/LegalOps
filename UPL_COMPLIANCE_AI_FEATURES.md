# UPL Compliance for Advanced AI Features

## 🚨 **UPL Risk Analysis: Advanced AI Features**

### **High-Risk Features:**
- ❌ **Contract analysis** (legal advice on contract terms)
- ❌ **Risk assessment** (legal risk evaluation)
- ❌ **Strategic business recommendations** (legal strategy advice)
- ❌ **Natural language queries** (legal advice in response to questions)

### **UPL Violation Risks:**
- **Providing legal advice** without being a licensed attorney
- **Analyzing legal documents** and providing legal opinions
- **Making legal recommendations** based on specific situations
- **Creating attorney-client relationship** through AI responses
