# Real Estate Package UPL Compliance Analysis

## 🚨 **UPL Risk Assessment: REAL ESTATE PACKAGES**

### **Overall UPL Risk Level: LOW-MEDIUM**
**Why Real Estate is Lower Risk:**
- Primarily administrative and document preparation services
- Clear separation between legal advice and document assistance
- Established precedent for non-attorney real estate services
- Strong disclaimers and attorney referral network
