# Business Formation: Current Pipeline vs. Expanded Suite

## 🔍 **Current Pipeline (What We're Building Now)**

### **Core Features (Phase 1 MVP):**
- ✅ **Entity Formation**: LLC, Corporation, Partnership, DBA formation workflows
- ✅ **AI-Guided Intake**: Intelligent form completion with real-time suggestions
- ✅ **Real-Time Sunbiz Integration**: Live name availability checking
- ✅ **Document Generation**: AI-powered operating agreements and filing forms
- ✅ **Compliance Tracking**: Basic deadline monitoring and reminders
- ✅ **Document Vault**: Secure storage with version control
- ✅ **Registered Agent Services**: Platform-managed and third-party options
- ✅ **Apple Glass Design**: Modern UI with glassmorphism effects

### **Current Scope:**
- **Geographic**: Florida only (Phase 1)
- **Entity Types**: LLC, Corp, Non-profit, LP, DBA
- **Filing Types**: Formation, Annual Reports, Amendments, Corrections
- **Compliance**: Basic tracking and reminders
- **Documents**: Operating agreements, bylaws, state forms

## 🚀 **Expanded Business Formation & Compliance Suite**

### **Enhanced Features (Beyond Current Pipeline):**

#### **1. Advanced Entity Management**
**Current**: Basic entity formation
**Expanded**: 
- **Multi-entity portfolio management** (manage 10+ entities from one dashboard)
- **Entity restructuring** (LLC to Corp conversions, mergers, acquisitions)
- **International entity formation** (Delaware, Nevada, Wyoming)
- **Entity optimization** (tax structure recommendations, jurisdiction analysis)

#### **2. Comprehensive Compliance Management**
**Current**: Basic deadline tracking
**Expanded**:
- **Multi-state compliance** (entities operating in multiple states)

- **Regulatory change monitoring** (automatic updates when laws change)
- **Compliance scoring** (risk assessment and improvement recommendations)
- **Audit preparation** (documentation for state and federal audits)

#### **3. Advanced Document Generation**
**Current**: Basic operating agreements and bylaws
**Expanded**:

- **Legal document library** (hundreds of templates for different scenarios)

#### **4. Financial Integration**
**Current**: Basic payment processing
**Expanded**:

#### **5. Business Intelligence & Analytics**
**Current**: Basic status tracking
**Expanded**:

#### **6. Advanced AI Features**
**Current**: Basic form completion and suggestions
**Expanded**:

#### **8. Multi-State Expansion**
**Current**: Florida only
